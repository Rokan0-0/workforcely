# Workforcely: Seamless Stage Script (5-Minute Dual Pitch)

This script is redesigned to be **short, punchy, and highly conversational**. It uses simple language to prevent tongue-twisters ("biting mouth") on stage.

- **Speaker A** (Business, Strategy, Close)
- **Speaker B** (Product, AI, Live Laptop Demos)

---

### Slide 1: Welcome to Workforcely

- **[Screen]**: Widescreen Title Slide
- **[Laptop in front of Judges]**: Credentials / Welcome screen
- **Speaker A**: "Good afternoon, judges. Think about how easy it is to pay for a ride or order food on your phone today. It's seamless. But behind the scenes, the businesses running our economy are still drowning in paper files, spreadsheets, and complex tax compliance. I'm [Speaker A Name], and this is my partner [Speaker B Name]. Today, we're showing you **Workforcely**—the operational engine for African SMEs."

---

### Slide 2: The Spreadsheet Nightmare

- **[Screen]**: Visual of messy spreadsheets vs. tax warning badges
- **Speaker A**: "In Nigeria, 39 million small businesses share one big headache: managing staff. Calculating monthly PAYE taxes by hand. Handling Pension deductions. Tracking attendance on paper. It is slow, it is messy, and it leads to heavy government audit fines. Small business owners waste up to 15 days a year just dealing with HR paperwork."

---

### Slide 3: Meet Workforcely

- **[Screen]**: High-level system overview (Lifecycle, Compliance, AI)
- **Speaker A**: "That is why we built Workforcely. We've consolidated the entire employee journey into one mobile-first app. Judges, look at the laptop in front of you. We have logged you in as **Olumide**, the HR Admin. Speaker B, take them through the system."

---

### Slide 4: Real-Time Self-Service & Local Taxes

- **[Screen]**: Employee dashboard, check-in screen, and payslip preview
- **[Laptop]**: Judges see Admin dashboard charts.
- **Speaker B**: "Judges, on the top-right toolbar of the laptop, click **Switch Role** and select **Employee: Fatima (Finance)**.
- _(Pause 2 seconds while they switch)_
- "Fatima has a custom portal. Right from her phone, she can tap **Clock In** to register her attendance. If she needs an official payslip, she navigates to **My Payslips** and clicks print. Our system automatically computes her exact progressive PAYE tax and Pension deductions based on current Nigerian tax laws. No manual HR calculations required."

---

### Slide 5: Hiring & Automating Onboarding

- **[Screen]**: Kanban Board and Onboarding checklist screenshots
- **[Laptop]**: Switch back to Admin.
- **Speaker B**: "Now, let’s switch back. In the topbar, switch your role back to **Admin: Olumide** and click **Recruitment & Jobs** on the sidebar.
- _(Pause 2 seconds)_
- "Hiring is hard, but onboarding is harder. With our visual Kanban board, you drag applicants from screen to screen. The moment you move a candidate to 'Hired', the system automatically creates their employee profile and builds a custom onboarding checklist. They can complete their tasks from their phone, updating HR in real-time."

---

### Slide 6: The AI HR Assistant

- **[Screen]**: Chat bubble showing: "Who is on leave next week?" -> Live Table
- **[Laptop]**: AI Assistant screen.
- **Speaker B**: "Now, let's look at the secret sauce. Click **AI HR Assistant** in the sidebar. We've connected our database directly to Google’s Gemini API. Forget running complex reports. Just ask. Click the suggest button: **'Who is on leave next week?'**."
- _(Pause 3 seconds while Gemini processes)_
- "In real-time, Gemini analyzed our leave table, checked the calendar context of June 2026, and returned a formatted table showing exactly who is off. You can ask it anything: from payroll totals to employee search queries."

### Slide 7: The Market Opportunity

- **[Screen]**: Widescreen Market Statistics (39M SMEs, $35B+ Salary volume)
- **Speaker A**: "This AI assistant turns hours of report building into a 3-second conversation. And the market opportunity is massive. We are targeting growing retail, fintech, and services teams who have outgrown WhatsApp groups and Excel, representing a multi-million dollar software market in West Africa."

### Slide 8: Simple Pricing Tiers

- **[Screen]**: Clean pricing cards (Free under 5, Premium, Enterprise)

As businesses grow, they unlock our premium tier at **N1,200 per employee per month**, which gives them automated payroll, advanced performance goals, and our Gemini AI Assistant."

### Slide 9: Our Roadmap

- **[Screen]**: Expansion timeline (Banking APIs, Automated Pension filing)
- **Speaker B**: "Our roadmap focuses on deep integrations. Next, we will connect directly with local banking APIs so HR admins can trigger direct, one-click salary payments straight from our payroll ledger."

---

### Slide 10: Let's Build the Future

- **[Screen]**: Call to action (Pitch email, website, Live App link)
- **Speaker A**: "Workforcely is not just an HR tool. It is the operating engine that helps emerging market businesses scale, stay compliant, and analyze their operations instantly. Judges, we invite you to keep clicking through the live demo. Thank you, and we are ready for your questions."

---

### 3 Tips for Stage Flow:

1. **Never Stop Speaking**: When Speaker B asks judges to click a button, Speaker A should maintain eye contact and keep the momentum going so there is no awkward silence.
2. **Keep Clicks Simple**: If a judge gets stuck on a page, don't sweat it. Focus on the screen behind you where the visual slide represents the exact same view.
3. **Pace Yourselves**: Speak slowly. 5 minutes is plenty of time for this conversational script. Let your natural chemistry shine through.
