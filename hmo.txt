Here is a comprehensive breakdown of what HMO (Health Maintenance Organization) & Healthcare Benefits is all about in Workforcely, why it matters for Nigerian businesses, and how to pitch it.

🏥 What is an HMO? (Background & Context)
In Nigeria and across Africa, HMO stands for Health Maintenance Organization. It is the corporate health insurance system that businesses provide to their staff, giving them access to pre-paid medical care across a network of private hospitals and clinics.

Leading HMO providers in Nigeria include Reliance HMO, Hygeia HMO, AXA Mansard Health, and Leadway Health.

❌ The Problem: Why HMO Management Is Painful for SMEs
Before Workforcely, HR administrators managed HMO benefits manually:

Paperwork & Email Chaos: Collecting physical forms or Excel files from employees to register their spouses and children.
Lack of Coverage Visibility: Employees rarely knew which hospitals they could visit or what their annual benefit caps were until an emergency occurred.
No Expense Oversight: HR had no real-time dashboard tracking monthly healthcare spending across departments.
